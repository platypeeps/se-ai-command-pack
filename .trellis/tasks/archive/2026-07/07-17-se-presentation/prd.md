# Implement se-presentation

## Goal

Transform an approved brief, article, proposal, or analysis into a coherent
presentation narrative and slide-level specification.

## Requirements

- Resolve audience, decision/outcome, duration, venue, source artifact, and constraints.
- Build story arc, slide sequence, one claim per slide, evidence, visual intent,
  speaker notes, transitions, and anticipated questions.
- Provide short/standard variants when requested without shrinking text blindly.
- Preserve citations and label unsupported visual/data suggestions.
- Hand actual deck production to presentation tooling.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Every slide advances the stated audience outcome.
- [ ] Evidence, visuals, and speaker claims are source-traceable.
- [ ] Tests cover executive/technical audiences, short duration, sparse evidence, and accessibility.
- [ ] Generated surfaces, documentation, release metadata, and full checks pass.

## Out of Scope

- Creating slide files, inventing charts, or presenting on the user's behalf.
