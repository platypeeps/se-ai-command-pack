# Implement se-stakeholder-map

## Goal

Map the people and groups relevant to a decision or initiative without presenting
speculation about their motives as fact.

## Requirements

- Resolve initiative, decision, scope, available evidence, and intended use.
- Record role, decision authority, influence, interests, concerns, information
  needs, dependencies, engagement sequence, and evidence/confidence.
- Separate observed positions, user-supplied judgment, and assistant inference.
- Highlight missing stakeholders, conflicting incentives, and validation questions.
- Avoid sensitive personal profiling or manipulative engagement recommendations.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Every inferred position is labeled and paired with a validation action.
- [ ] Formal authority and informal influence remain distinct.
- [ ] Tests cover unknown stakeholders, conflicting roles, private data, and organizational change.
- [ ] Generated surfaces, documentation, release metadata, and full checks pass.

## Out of Scope

- Contacting people, political profiling, or asserting private motivations.
