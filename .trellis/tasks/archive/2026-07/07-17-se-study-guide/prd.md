# Implement se-study-guide

## Goal

Transform a supplied source set into material optimized for understanding,
retrieval, and practice rather than ordinary summarization.

## Requirements

- Inventory and fully cover supplied sources or disclose unreadable sections.
- Produce concept map, essential definitions, worked examples, common traps,
  retrieval questions, flashcard-ready prompts, practice problems, and review order.
- Preserve source attribution and distinguish generated exercises from source content.
- Adjust difficulty and assumed prerequisites for the stated learner.
- Avoid duplicating `se-distill`: retention and application control what is kept.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Questions test recall, explanation, application, and misconception repair.
- [ ] Generated answers remain traceable to sources or labeled inference.
- [ ] Tests cover thin sources, conflicting definitions, technical notation, and injection safety.
- [ ] Generated surfaces, documentation, release metadata, and full checks pass.

## Out of Scope

- Teaching sessions, external research without approval, or certification.
