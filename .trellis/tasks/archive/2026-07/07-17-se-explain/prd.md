# Implement se-explain

## Goal

Explain a complex supplied topic accurately at the depth and vocabulary needed
by a stated audience.

## Requirements

- Resolve topic, audience, purpose, prior knowledge, and desired depth.
- Layer the response through concise model, intuition, example, mechanism,
  limitations, misconceptions, and next learning step as appropriate.
- Mark analogies as analogies and identify where simplification stops being accurate.
- Cite or route unsupported/current claims to research rather than inventing authority.
- Support follow-up questions and progressive depth without repeating the full explanation.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The explanation is visibly adapted to audience and purpose.
- [ ] Analogies, facts, assumptions, and limitations remain distinct.
- [ ] Tests cover novice/expert audiences, false premises, current claims, and injection safety.
- [ ] Generated surfaces, documentation, release metadata, and full checks pass.

## Out of Scope

- Full curricula, mastery assessment, or original research.
