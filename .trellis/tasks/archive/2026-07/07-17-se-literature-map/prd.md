# Implement se-literature-map

## Goal

Map the intellectual structure of a defined research field or question rather
than collapsing it into a single narrative review.

## Requirements

- Define scope, dates, disciplines, source types, and inclusion/exclusion rules.
- Identify foundational and recent works, schools, methods, citation/response
  relationships, agreements, disputes, gaps, and open questions.
- Distinguish influential work from strong evidence and disclosed coverage from completeness.
- Produce a justified reading sequence for the user's purpose.
- Reuse source standards and hand deeper synthesis to `se-research` or `se-paper`.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Every cluster and relationship is source-traceable and dated.
- [ ] Search limitations and missing databases are explicit.
- [ ] Tests cover fragmented fields, contradictory schools, inaccessible works, and citation errors.
- [ ] Generated surfaces, documentation, release metadata, and full checks pass.

## Out of Scope

- Bibliometric guarantees, paper drafting, or claiming exhaustive coverage.
