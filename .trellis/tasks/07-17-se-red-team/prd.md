# Implement se-red-team

## Goal

Challenge a proposal, decision, article, conclusion, or plan with the strongest
relevant adversarial analysis while remaining evidence-based and constructive.

## Requirements

- Resolve artifact, intended outcome, threat/adversary frame, constraints, and review depth.
- Test hidden assumptions, contrary evidence, incentives, misuse/abuse, failure
  modes, security/privacy, strongest counterargument, and reversal conditions.
- Separate demonstrated defects, plausible risks, speculative cases, and value disagreements.
- Steelman before criticizing and include mitigations or evidence needed to resolve findings.
- Avoid duplicating claim-level `se-fact-check` or rubric-based `se-evaluate`.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Findings include severity, evidence, uncertainty, consequence, and response.
- [ ] The review cannot invent adversaries or vulnerabilities as facts.
- [ ] Tests cover strong artifacts, weak assumptions, sensitive security detail, and no findings.
- [ ] Generated surfaces, documentation, release metadata, and full checks pass.

## Out of Scope

- Offensive operations, harassment, final approval, or implementing mitigations.
