# Implement se-socratic-review

## Goal

Assess and deepen understanding through an adaptive dialogue that asks one
question at a time and bases conclusions on demonstrated reasoning.

## Requirements

- Resolve topic, target level, purpose, source curriculum, and session bounds.
- Ask exactly one question per turn and adapt difficulty from the answer.
- Require commitment before revealing the explanation unless the user asks to stop.
- Diagnose conceptual, procedural, and transfer gaps without humiliating language.
- End with evidence-based capability, misconception, and next-practice report.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Difficulty and follow-ups respond to demonstrated performance.
- [ ] The skill does not leak answers through leading questions by default.
- [ ] Tests cover correct guesses, partial reasoning, persistent misconceptions, and stopping.
- [ ] Generated surfaces, documentation, release metadata, and full checks pass.

## Out of Scope

- Formal grading, credentialing, adversarial interviewing, or psychological assessment.
