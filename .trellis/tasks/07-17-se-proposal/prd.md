# Implement se-proposal

## Goal

Develop a persuasive, decision-ready proposal that earns agreement for a
technical, operational, or business intervention.

## Requirements

- Resolve audience, decision authority, problem, current cost, desired outcome,
  constraints, alternatives, evidence, investment, risks, and explicit ask.
- Interview for firsthand context and stakeholder objections before drafting.
- Distinguish evidence, estimates, assumptions, and advocacy.
- Include alternatives and a credible do-nothing baseline.
- Hand an accepted proposal to `se-plan`; do not imply approval or create work records.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The proposal names the decision and evidence required from its audience.
- [ ] Costs, benefits, and commitments cannot be fabricated.
- [ ] Tests cover weak evidence, multiple stakeholders, missing authority, and rejected framing.
- [ ] Generated surfaces, documentation, release metadata, and full checks pass.

## Out of Scope

- Approval, negotiation, task creation, or implementation planning.
