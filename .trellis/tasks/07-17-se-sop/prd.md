# Implement se-sop

## Goal

Document a routine, repeatable process with enough control and evidence for
consistent execution and maintenance.

## Requirements

- Resolve purpose, scope, trigger/frequency, inputs, roles, procedure, outputs,
  controls, records, exceptions, escalation, and review owner.
- Base the procedure on observed/approved practice and label proposed improvements.
- Separate mandatory controls from helpful guidance.
- Include version, effective date, review cadence, and change history expectations.
- Route event-driven failure/recovery procedures to `se-runbook`.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Roles, inputs, outputs, controls, and exceptions are operationally testable.
- [ ] Current practice and proposed future state remain distinct.
- [ ] Tests cover undocumented exceptions, conflicting sources, compliance claims, and stale procedures.
- [ ] Generated surfaces, documentation, release metadata, and full checks pass.

## Out of Scope

- Process enforcement, staff assignment, or policy approval.
